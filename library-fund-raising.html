Fund-Raising for the New Shutesbury Library and Community Center

  • Private fund-raising for the new Shutesbury Library and Community Center is dramatically lowering how much the town will need to borrow.

    If we reach our fund-raising goal of $400,000 we will have lowered the cost in taxes for an average Shutesbury home from $1.98 a week to just $1.41 a week.

    The average cost per week is based on the total increase in taxes for the average home in Shutesbury, $243,814 averaged over the 22 year life of the proposed loan, 2013-2034.

    The cost to taxpayers will also drop if the town spends less than the current estimate of $1,400,000 to build the library.
