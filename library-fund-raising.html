Fundraising for the new Shutesbury Library and Community Center reduces the tax increase for everybody!

  • Private fund-raising for the new Shutesbury Library and Community Center is dramatically lowering how much the town will need to borrow.

    When we reach our fund-raising goal of $400,000 the cost in additional taxes for the average Shutesbury household will have dropped almost 30% from $8.59 to just $6.14 per month.

    The average cost per month is calculated on the total increase in taxes for the average home in Shutesbury valued at $243,814 averaged over the 22 year life of the proposed loan from 2013 to 2034.

    There will be additional savings to taxpayers if we spend less than $1,400,000 to build the library by using volunteers and donated materials.
