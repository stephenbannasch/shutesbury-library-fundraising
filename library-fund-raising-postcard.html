Raised for Library Lowers Everyone's Tax Costs

  • Fundraising is dramatically reducing what Shutesbury needs to borrow to build our new Library and Community Center.

    When fundraising reaches $400,000 the additional tax on the average property in Shutesbury assessed at $243,814 will have dropped almost 30% from $8.60 to just $6.14 per month.

    You can estimate what your additional tax costs will be averaged over the 22 year term of the loan by selecting the assessment closest to your property value from the table below:

    There will be additional savings to taxpayers if we spend less than the current estimate of $1,400,000 needed to build the library by using volunteers and donated materials.

    Help make the new Shutesbury Library and Community Center a reality by talking with your neighbors, making a donation and joining the http://YesForLibrary.com group!
