Raised for Library Lowers Everyone's Tax Costs

  • Fundraising is dramatically reducing what Shutesbury needs to borrow to build our new Library and Community Center.

    Based on the $160,424 raised so far, you can estimate what your average additional tax costs will be over the 22 year term of the loan by changing the selections below:

    • Assessed Value
    • Fundraising

    see: MultivalueTax-1M.pdf.

    Raising $400,000 drops the additional monthly tax on the average property in Shutesbury (assessed at $243,814) almost 30% from $8.60 to just $6.14.

    There will be additional savings to taxpayers if we spend less than $1,400,000 to build the library by using volunteers and donated materials.
